Naawi Naawi-Oodena



Naawi-Oodena is a fully developed urban reserve in Winnipeg, built on the former Kapyong Barracks lands by the seven Treaty One Nations. It features housing, commercial space, cultural centres, and green areas designed to reflect Indigenous values and self-governance. As the largest urban Indigenous development in Canada, it serves as a powerful symbol of reconciliation and economic renewal. Naawi-Oodena blends modern city living with deep cultural roots, creating a vibrant, self-sustaining Indigenous community.
